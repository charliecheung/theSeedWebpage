― About Us ―

The Mustard Seed U of C Chapter is a student organization at the University of
Calgary representing The Mustard Seed.
Working together, we strive to accomplish our shared vision of

Building community, growing hope, and supporting change

We aim to combat poverty at its root by supporting the goal of
The Mustard Seed - meeting the basic needs of shelter, food and clothing.
We encourage and provide acceptance to those experiencing poverty
and strive to help those in need find sustainable housing and employment.
We offer regular individual and group volunteering opportunities both at
The Mustard Seed and the University of Calgary. You can also help out during
fundraisers throughout the year and advocate
for The Mustard Seed and the issues we face as a community.

― Volunteer ―

― On campus ―

We offer a variety of on campus activities throughout the year
including bake sales, fundraisers, and awareness events. Choose an event that
suits your schedule and earn credits towards your CCR while also helping out the community!

― Off campus ―

We offer a variety of activities at the Mustard Seed locations every two weeks
including donation sorting and meal prep. Choose an event that suits your schedule
and earn credits towards your CCR while also helping out the community!
